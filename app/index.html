Help
How to use Glacier — everything explained.

🏠 Today Tab

Your daily command center. The Right Now card shows your current task with a countdown to the next one. Quick log boxes are one-tap habit checks. The timeline is your full daily schedule — tap any task to mark it done. Tap the priority dot (left of each task) to cycle: none → low → medium → high.

✅ Habits Tab

Tap any habit box to check it off for the day. Staying clean streaks: tap to add +1 day, tap & hold (half second) to reset to zero. Use ＋ Add buttons to create custom habits or streaks. Tap the pencil icon on any habit to edit its emoji, name, or description — or delete it.

📊 Stats Tab

Your all-time scoreboard. Today's Score shows current completion. The 🔥 streak counts consecutive days where you hit ≥50% of your habits. The heatmap shows 30 days of activity. All records update automatically as you check things off.

⚖️ Body Tab

Log your weight each morning. The trend chart shows your progress toward your goal. Pace estimates when you'll hit your target at the current rate. Tap Goal to change your target weight.

📝 Notes

Rich text notes with bold, italic, colors, sizes, and alignment. Notes are saved on this device. Tap a note to edit it.

🖼️ Inspo

Upload images for your vision board. Images are compressed and stored locally on this device in the browser's storage (~5 MB limit). They persist between sessions but won't sync to other devices.

⭐ Levels & XP

Tap the LV badge in the header to see your level details. You earn XP by checking habits (+40 XP each) and completing tasks (+20 XP each). Every 800 XP = 1 level up. Levels go from Rookie (LV 1) all the way to GOAT (LV 16+).

🔔 Notifications

Glacier sends branded push notifications that follow your own schedule — any task with the 🔔 toggle on gets a push at its time, in your timezone, even when the app is closed. Setup: (1) on iPhone, add Glacier to your home screen (desktop browsers work directly), (2) tap Set up on the Reminders card and allow notifications, (3) be signed in on the Sync page. Every signed-in device you turn on gets reminders.

⚡ Learn

Pick topics you care about and Glacier serves one tip a day, rotating through the library — plus a 9 AM push if reminders are on. Use the search box to pull any tip on demand.
